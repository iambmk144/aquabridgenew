Who we are

Revolutionizing the shrimp supply chain, one harvest at a time.

At Aqua Bridge, our mission is to empower shrimp farmers by solving critical harvesting and market-linkage challenges. We believe in a fair, efficient, and transparent ecosystem where farmers get the value they deserve for their hard work.

We leverage technology and a strong network to bridge the gap between farmers and buyers — streamlining the post-harvest process and guaranteeing financial security for the farming community.
